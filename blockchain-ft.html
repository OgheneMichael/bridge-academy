Participation in the full-time program is a ~50 hour per week comittment over 19 weeks.

Online - anywhere in the world. The majority of the program can be taken asynchonously with office hours being one of the main synchronous components.

PAYMENT OPTIONS

1. $0 up-front + $18,000 maximum repayment via an ISA agreement, repaid over time. See FAQ for details.

PRE-REQUISITES

If you have previous blockchain development experience you can skip over the pre-reqs, otherwise you should join our crash course to get up to speed. You'll need to prove mastery of these topics before being accepted.

Weeks 1 - 4: Building a Foundation

During this period we will go over all of the pre-training curriculum in depth to test for mastery as well as introduce new more advanced material. This will be extremely fast paced as you should be familiar with each many of the topics before hand.

Week 5: Capstone Project Proposal

During this phase you will come up with your capstone project proposal along side one of our technology partner projects. Final matches are made, projects are assigned and a project kick-off will be scheduled.

Weeks 6-16: Apprenticeship & Capstone Project

During this phase you will work on your capstone project, getting continuous feedback from your mentor. There will also be some coursework during this time, but it will be light.

Week 16: Demo Day & Project Launch

During this time you'll launch your project to the world and start preparing your resume and portfolio for reaching out to companies. We will also help with job interview prep, career coaching and making introductions through our hiring partner network.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Bridge a Coding Bootcamp?

No. Our program is built for experienced developers, looking to gain new skills and uplevel their careers.

How long does the program last?

The full-time program lasts for twelve weeks. New batches open up each month.

How big of a time commitment is it?

The full-time program is commitment of ~45hrs per week.

Why is there a deposit required?

We ask for a $100 USD (7,100 INR) deposit to secure your spot once you are accepted. Your deposit will be refunded after you complete the first program phase.

How much do I pay in total?

You will never pay more than the total $18,000 USD cap (1,278,000 INR). Payments are made month by month (10% of your salary) for 36 months.

How do the monthly payments work?

If you do not earn more than $1,760 USD (125,000 INR) in a given month, we don't collect any payments for that month.

Can I see an example ISA?

Yes, you can see a template we use here. ISAs are not a loan so they have no interest and are paid back over 36 months.

How much scholarship funds should I expect to receive?

The average applicant receives ~$1,500 USD (106,507 INR) in scholarship funds. This amount is deducted from the program fees you owe.

What are the most commons forms of scholarships?

Our technology partners make up over 50% of scholarships with Projet earnings making ~25%.

How do I know if i'm qualified to join?

Please read the specific track requirements before applying. Most candidates have at least 2+ years of programming experience w/ general web or mobile development.

Can I enroll from anywhere?

As long as you have a reliable internet connection to join in on weekly office hours calls with your program mentor.

Can I participate while employed?

No. We highly discourage you from working or freelancing or attending university while attending the Bridge Full-time program.

Do I need previous Blockchain development experience to apply?

No! We do ask that you participate in our crash course before joining to get up to speed before joining.

I’d like to start my own company. Should I apply?

It depends, but if your primary interest is starting a company, you should apply to Y Combinator.

How many Bridge applicants come from outside the U.S?

Over 50% of Bridge applicants come from outside the U.S. We strongly encourage International applicants to apply.
