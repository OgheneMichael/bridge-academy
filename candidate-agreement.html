Candidate Income Share Agreement

Prism Labs Inc (referred to here as “we”, “our” and “BridgeAcademy”) is a company that operates a mentorship and technical training program. Our services and mentor network may be accessed by users (referred to here as “you”, “your”, and “Candidate”) interested in receiving training from BridgeAcademy and mentorship from professional developers in exchange for a share of their future earnings.

WHEREAS Candidate desires to use BridgeAcademy’s online platform and services (“the Services”) to access online training materials and engage with one or more potential mentors (each a “Mentor”), these Mentors having entered into a separate agreement with BridgeAcademy;

AND WHEREAS BridgeAcademy desires to grant Candidate access to the Services under the terms and conditions of the present Agreement;

NOW THEREFORE in consideration of the mutual promises contained herein and for other good and valuable consideration, BridgeAcademy and Candidate, intending to be legally bound, do agree as follows:

1. Definitions

A. “Mentorship” means supervision and guidance provided by Mentor to one or more Candidates, under the terms described in Section 2. Mentorship shall consist of teaching, debugging, and the recommendation of appropriate learning resources and software packages to Candidates, and other activities whose purpose is to develop Candidates’ employable skills in blockchain, web development, machine learning, data science or similar fields. Mentorship may be one-on-one, or may involve a higher Candidate-to-Mentor ratio. Mentorship may also include support in interview preparation or networking.

B. “Weekly Mentorship Commitment” means the minimum number of hours that Mentor must be available to each Candidate for mentorship, whether individually or in groups. Unless otherwise agreed to in writing by Mentor, BridgeAcademy, and the Candidate(s) receiving Mentorship, the Weekly Mentorship Commitment shall be specified in an Offer of Mentorship.

C. “Formal Mentorship Period” means the period of time during which Mentor is required to make themselves available to fulfil their Weekly Mentorship Commitment. Unless otherwise agreed to in writing by Mentor, BridgeAcademy, and the Candidate(s) receiving Mentorship, the Formal Mentorship Period shall be specified in an Offer of Mentorship.

G. “Mentorship Start Date” means a date mutually agreed upon by BridgeAcademy, Mentor and Candidates to whom Mentor has agreed to provide Mentorship, upon which the Formal Mentorship Period shall begin.

H. “Eligible Field” means a domain or discipline that makes use of the skillset developed by a Candidate during their Mentorship. This includes but is not limited to blockchain, web development, machine learning or data science. BridgeAcademy shall exercise its discretion to determine whether a particular Candidate’s domain of employment qualifies as an Eligible Field for the purpose of this Agreement.

I. “Prism Labs Income Share Percentage” is the percentage of Candidate's salary that shall be owed to BridgeAcademy in exchange for Candidate's use of the Platform in connection with a Mentorship. Unless otherwise specified in writing, the Prism Labs Income Share Percentage shall be equal to % of Candidate's salary, plus applicable taxes and transaction fees, for the first 36 months of Candidate's employment in an Eligible Field.

J. "Monthly Payment" means your Income Share times the amount of your Qualified Monthly Earned Income.

K. “Payment Obligation” is the total amount of payment due from each monthly Payment over the total Payment Term, unless otherwise agreed to in writing by BridgeAcademy and the Candidate(s).

L. “Payment Cap" is the maximum amount that a Candidate will be paying back over the course of the Payment Term.

M. "Payment Term" means the term during which you pay us a fixed percentage of your Earned Income.

N. “Offer of Mentorship” is a formal offer of mentorship extended between BridgeAcademy and the Mentor.

2. Mentorship of and engagement with mentors

J. In the event that Mentor is unable to fulfill the Weekly Mentorship Commitment on any particular week during the Formal Mentorship Period, Mentor will offer additional Mentorship in subsequent weeks to compensate.

K. If Mentor has fulfilled their Weekly Mentorship Commitment with respect to a particular Candidate throughout the Formal Mentorship Period, BridgeAcademy shall be entitled to a sum equal the Payment Obligation, plus applicable taxes and transaction fees.

L. Candidate shall not owe the Payment Obligation unless Candidate becomes employed in an Eligible Field within two (2) years following the conclusion of the Formal Mentorship Period (hereafter, the "Repayment Period"), in an Eligible Field.

M. In the event that Candidate becomes employed during the Formal Mentorship Period, Candidate shall still owe the full Payment Obligation, subject to any adjustments determined by BridgeAcademy in its sole and exclusive discretion.

N. In the event that Candidate becomes employed in an Eligible Field during the Repayment Period, but subsequently loses that employment, Candidate shall not owe the Payment Obligation during the months Candidate is not employed. Notwithstanding the foregoing, if such a Candidate, having lost their earlier employment, should subsequently, and within the Repayment Period, find employment in an Eligible Field, Candidate shall owe the Payment Obligation associated with Candidate's new position. For clarity, the Payment Obligation shall be calculated based on the salary earned by Candidate in respect of each applicable employment for a total of 36 months.

3. Payment schedule

A. The Payment Obligation shall be payable starting on the first day of the Candidate’s employment in an Eligible Field, as follows:

B. Candidate hereby agrees to submit, within one month of hire, to BridgeAcademy evidence of employment salary. The forms acceptable to Prism Labs Include, but are not limited to, signed employment agreement, employer pay stub and T4 slips (collectively, "Proof of Salary"). Unless otherwise specified in writing between BridgeAcademy and Candidate, in the event that Candidate fails to provide Proof of Salary within 30 days, Candidate agrees that BridgeAcademy, in its sole discretion, may determine the Payment Obligation. Candidate further agrees to submit their credit card, or other payment information to BridgeAcademy as collateral for enforcement of this Agreement.

C. Candidate hereby agrees that, at BridgeAcademy request, Candidate shall make available to BridgeAcademy Candidate's tax filings for a period from the beginning of the Formal Mentorship Period, to up to three (3) years following the end of the Formal Mentorship Period, which may be used by BridgeAcademy as evidence of Candidate's employment status for the purpose of enforcing this Agreement.

D. BridgeAcademy and Candidate's Mentor may, at their sole and exclusive discretion, offer to defer payment for Candidate's employment until some pre-determined milestone has been reached (for example, Candidate's promotion to full-time status, Candidate's being offered a raise, or at an agreed-upon date). In the event of such a deferral, the salary used for the purpose of calculating the Payment Obligation owed to BridgeAcademy shall be Candidate's salary as of the deferred payment date. Specifically, if Candidate's salary increases between the date on which their employment begins, and the date to which their repayment has been deferred, then the new, higher salary will be used, and Candidate shall owe the corresponding, increased Payment Obligation.

D. Term. This Agreement shall enter into effect at the date on which it is executed by both BridgeAcademy and Candidate (the “Effective Date”) and shall continue until any and all payments required to pay in accordance with the agreement have been paid (the “Term”).”

E. Termination. Either party may terminate the present Agreement at any time, provided that no Formal Mentorship Periods associated with Candidate are outstanding at the time of such termination. The following provisions shall survive termination of this Agreement for a period of five (5) years: Section 4. Upon termination of this Agreement, Candidate agrees to continue payments of all Payment Obligations due or accrued under this Agreement as of the date of such termination according to the payment schedule of Section 3.

Neither party shall have the right to assign this Agreement to another party without the other party's written consent, except that BridgeAcademy may, upon written notice to Candidate (and without Candidate's consent) assign this Agreement in its entirety to a parent company, any subsidiary of a parent company, or an assignee in connection with a corporate reorganization, acquisition, merger, or sale of substantially all of its assets; provided that the assignee agrees in writing to be bound by all of the terms and conditions of this Agreement.

No waiver by BridgeAcademy or Candidate of any right under this Agreement shall be construed as a waiver of any other right. Neither BridgeAcademy nor Candidate shall be required to give notice to enforce strict adherence to all terms of this Agreement.

No modification of or amendment to this Agreement, nor any waiver of any rights under this Agreement, shall be effective unless agreed to in writing and signed by both parties.
